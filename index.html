Amir and his family like to spend leisure time at Titiwangsa Lake Park on weekends. They usually jog around the lake in the morning. After that, they will have a picnic under the shady trees. Pelajar A: Apa aktiviti kegemaran kamu?

Pelajar B: Saya suka bermain badminton. Bagaimana dengan kamu?
Student B: I like to play badminton. How about you?

Pelajar B: Wah, menarik! Bila kamu biasanya menonton filem?

Student A: On weekends. This activity makes us closer.
