Petikan:
Amir dan keluarganya suka beriadah di Taman Tasik Titiwangsa pada hujung minggu. Mereka biasanya berjoging di sekeliling tasik pada waktu pagi. Selepas itu, mereka akan berkelah di bawah pokok yang rindang. Adik Amir suka bermain buaian manakala ibunya gemar membaca buku. Ayahnya pula suka memancing di tepi tasik. Aktiviti ini membuatkan keluarga mereka lebih rapat dan sihat.
Passage:
Amir and his family like to spend leisure time at Titiwangsa Lake Park on weekends. They usually jog around the lake in the morning. After that, they will have a picnic under the shady trees. Amir's sister likes to play on the swings while his mother enjoys reading books. His father likes to fish by the lake. These activities make their family closer and healthier.

Dialog ringkas tentang aktiviti kegemaran
Short dialogue about favorite activities

Dialog / Dialogue

Pelajar A: Apa aktiviti kegemaran kamu?
Student A: What is your favorite activity?
Pelajar B: Saya suka bermain badminton. Bagaimana dengan kamu?
Student B: I like to play badminton. How about you?
Pelajar A: Saya gemar menonton filem dengan keluarga.
Student A: I enjoy watching movies with my family.
Pelajar B: Wah, menarik! Bila kamu biasanya menonton filem?
Student B: Wow, interesting! When do you usually watch movies?
Pelajar A: Pada hujung minggu. Aktiviti ini membuatkan kami lebih rapat.
Student A: On weekends. This activity makes us closer.
